Introducing Addis Digital Consultancy

Addis Digital Consultancy offers a specialised digital consulting service targeted at African businesses looking to transform and grow their businesses digitally. Founded by digital natives and practitioner returnees from Europe and US, Addis Digital Consultancy based in Ethiopia, has a focused approach to digitisation that has a tangible impact on the emerging digital economy in Africa. Through our 6 core practice areas we aim to tackle key digital challenges faced by businesses in Africa and particularly Ethiopia.

Why Do We Exist
